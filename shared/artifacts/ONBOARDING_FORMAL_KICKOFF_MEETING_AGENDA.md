# TGIF Phase 2 — Franchise Group Formal Kickoff Meeting Agenda (Onboarding)

## Purpose
The purpose of this meeting is to align on the franchise group rollout approach and confirm the owners, dates, and required inputs needed to support a successful go-live.

This meeting produces an agreed onboarding plan for this franchise group, including:
- Rollout dates and location list
- Contacts + roles
- System integration approach (accounting/payroll)
- Hardware/site survey plan
- Training + communication plan
- Issue routing + escalation
- Cadence and next milestones

## Logistics
- **Meeting length:** 60 minutes (extend to 75 if integrations are complex)
- **Meeting owner/facilitator:** HCSS (Project Management)
- **Notes owner:** Fridays PMO / HCSS (assigned in meeting)
- **Working artifacts:**
  - Franchise Group Contact Table (shared sheet)
  - System Integrations Tracking Sheet (shared sheet)
  - Rollout Tracking Sheet (shared sheet)

## Required Attendees (Franchise Group)
- **Primary business contact** (decision-maker)
- **Operations leader** (rollout owner)
- **IT/Technical contact** (SFTP/email/file transfer, networking)
- **Accounting/Finance contact** (GL mapping, imports)
- **Payroll/HR contact** (employee IDs, pay period, time import)
- Optional: **Training lead** and/or a representative GM

## Required Attendees (TGIF / Vendors)
- **Fridays operations lead**
- **Fridays IT lead / Help Desk representative**
- **Toast implementation contact**
- **Crunchtime implementation contact**
- **Hardware/logistics lead** (site surveys, shipping, installs)
- **HCSS PM/Analyst support**

## Pre-Work (send 48 hours before)
1. **Franchise group completes:**
   - Confirm the contact list (names, emails, phones)
   - Provide list of locations in scope (store name/number, address, timezone)
   - Confirm accounting system + payroll system
   - Confirm file transfer constraints (SFTP available? email only? portal upload?)
2. **Fridays/HCSS prepares:**
   - Draft wave assignment + proposed go-live sequence
   - Draft training schedule options
   - Draft site survey + hardware timeline

## Agenda

### 1) Introductions + Meeting Outcome (5 min)
- Confirm attendees and decision-makers
- Confirm the purpose and the required outputs for the end of the call

### 2) Rollout Scope + Dates (10 min)
- Confirm franchise group location count and the list of stores in scope
- Confirm wave assignment and target go-live sequence
- Confirm any date constraints (holidays, local events, staffing)

**Decisions required:**
- Target go-live start date
- Store sequence (pilot store, then rollout order)

### 3) Franchise Group Profile (5 min)
- Confirm how this group operates day-to-day
- Confirm store leadership structure (who owns readiness at each store)

**Decisions required:**
- Primary rollout owner for franchise group
- Store-level owner pattern (GM vs multi-unit lead)

### 4) Systems + Integrations (Accounting / Payroll) (15–20 min)
- Confirm accounting system, payroll system, and any constraints
- Confirm whether native connectors exist vs flat file process
- Confirm required inputs:
  - Chart of accounts
  - GL mapping owner
  - Pay period schedule
  - Employee ID strategy
- Confirm file delivery method and frequency

**Decisions required:**
- Integration approach (native connector vs flat file vs temporary manual)
- File delivery method (SFTP / email / portal)
- Who signs off on GL mapping and payroll mapping

### 5) Hardware + Site Surveys (10 min)
- Confirm site survey schedule and prerequisites
- Confirm shipping address per store and receiving constraints
- Confirm install approach (onsite support needs, timing, access)

**Decisions required:**
- Site survey date(s)
- Hardware delivery and install plan

### 6) Training + Communication (10 min)
- Confirm training audiences (management, FOH)
- Confirm training delivery method (remote vs onsite)
- Confirm timeline expectations and required attendance
- Confirm communication path to stores (who receives comms)

**Decisions required:**
- Training schedule and required participants
- Communication recipients and method

### 7) Issue Routing + Governance (5 min)
- Confirm where issues go first and how they are tracked
- Confirm escalation path and response expectations
- Confirm meeting cadence and working sessions

**Standards:**
- All issues/questions should be routed through the designated support channel (help desk / agreed alias) so they can be tracked and resolved.

**Decisions required:**
- Issue intake channel
- Escalation path
- Cadence (bi-weekly working sessions + regular PM check-in)

### 8) Next Steps + Commitments (5 min)
- Review action items by owner with due dates
- Confirm next meeting(s) and what will be ready by then

## Outputs (what must exist after this meeting)
- Confirmed **Franchise Group Contact Table** (complete)
- Confirmed **Location list** (store name/number/address/timezone)
- Confirmed **Integration approach** (accounting + payroll)
- Confirmed **hardware/site survey plan**
- Confirmed **training + communication plan**
- Confirmed **issue routing + escalation**
- Confirmed **next 2 milestones** with dates
